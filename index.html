Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 22, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Retro File Manager ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our iOS application.

1. Information We Collect

Retro File Manager is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data to our servers. The app only accesses the data you explicitly authorize for the functionality you choose to use.

OAuth Authentication Data

If you choose to connect your cloud storage accounts, the app may request:

  • Google Drive: OAuth tokens to access your Google Drive files
  • Yandex Disk: OAuth tokens to access your Yandex Disk files

These tokens are stored locally on your device and are used solely to authenticate your access to your own cloud storage files.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information accessed through OAuth authentication is used exclusively for:

  • Allowing you to browse, view, and manage your files in connected cloud storage services
  • Enabling file operations you initiate (upload, download, delete, rename, etc.)
  • Maintaining your authenticated session with cloud storage providers

We do not:

  • Collect or store your files on our servers
  • Access your cloud storage without your explicit permission
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Use your data for analytics, advertising, or any purpose other than providing app functionality

3. Third-Party Services

Retro File Manager integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Google Sign-In for iOS: For authenticating with Google Drive
  • Google Drive API: For accessing and managing your Google Drive files
  • Yandex OAuth API: For authenticating with Yandex Disk
  • Yandex Disk REST API: For accessing and managing your Yandex Disk files

When you use these services, their respective privacy policies apply:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Yandex Privacy Policy

4. Data Storage and Security

All authentication tokens and app data are stored locally on your device using iOS secure storage mechanisms (Keychain). We implement industry-standard security practices to protect your data:

  • OAuth tokens are stored in the iOS Keychain
  • All communications with cloud services use HTTPS encryption
  • No data is transmitted to or stored on our servers

5. Data Retention

Since we do not collect or store your data on our servers, we do not retain any of your personal information. Authentication tokens remain on your device until you:

  • Disconnect a cloud storage account within the app
  • Delete the app from your device

6. Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: You can access your cloud storage files through the respective service providers
  • Deletion: You can disconnect cloud storage accounts at any time within the app settings
  • Revocation: You can revoke app permissions through your Google or Yandex account settings

7. Children's Privacy

Retro File Manager does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is not directed at children, and we do not intentionally gather data from users under the age of 13.

8. International Data Transfers

Your data remains on your device and on the cloud storage services you choose to connect. We comply with applicable data protection regulations including GDPR (EU) and CCPA (California).

9. Advertising and Analytics

Retro File Manager does not contain any advertisements or analytics tracking. We do not collect usage statistics or behavioral data.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
